About Me

I work to develop products that embody originality, priortize user epxerience & redefine norms.

Hello! I'm Toritse-Valerie. I'm all about bringing unique ideas to life, making sure they're not just beautiful, but also user-friendly. My journey began with a User Experience course at BrainStation and a Design course at the University of Toronto. This drew me to the realms of product design and web development. I've since earned a Front End Development certificate at Juno College of Technology & a Bachelor of Arts in Digital Enterprise Management at the University of Toronto.

My approach is rooted in understanding the story behind every project. Research, design, strategy – that's my trio. I want to ensure that whatever I craft tells your story just right. Away from the screen, I dive into painting, drawing, and sports. It's this balance of tech and art that keeps my creativity flowing.

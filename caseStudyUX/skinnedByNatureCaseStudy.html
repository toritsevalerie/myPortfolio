How might we create an easy, interactive and personalized skin care experience catered to each user?

Skinned By Nature

Product Design | Branding | Case Study | Freelance

Client

Skinned by Nature

Roles & Responsibilities

Product Designer, Product Strategy, User Research, Interaction, Visual design,

Project Duration

5 Months

Project Overview

The Product

Skinned, By Nature, is a unisex skincare line aimed at helping everyday people improve the health of their skin through natural skin care products. This handcrafted skincare line is aimed at making customers feel fresh and rejuvenated using natural and organic ingredients from all over the world.

The Problem

  • The Lack of accessibility features (specifically related to visual impairment and alternative text)
  • No user-centred experience
  • No personalized experience that caters to each customer's needs
  • No account personalization options (wishlists, saved orders, “see more” features, profile pages, etc.)
  • Overly simplified product detail page
  • Limited Interactive Features
  • Plain user interface excessive whitespace
  • Overly simplified design
  • Too many steps checkout out steps
  • No mobile app is available

Goal

To redesign the old company website and build a new mobile app while creating a tailored user-centred skin care experience unique to each customer. Improve current website design and its ease of use. Implement the inclusion of interactive features, and prioritize accessibility features catered towards visual impairment. The overarching goal is to let users control their own shopping experience with features that cater to their individual needs.
